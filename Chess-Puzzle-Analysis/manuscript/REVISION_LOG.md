## v5 (2026-03-12) — Strategic Reframing

**Rationale:** N=100 is below GMM recommended minimum (200; Nylund et al., 2007). GMM results must be framed as exploratory. Additionally, 2 of 5 trajectory patterns (Oscillating, ABE) were identified through EdNet exploratory analysis, not derived purely from theory. Manuscript was overclaiming confirmatory status.

**Changes (10 edits):**

1. **Abstract** — Rewrote to frame LCGA as primary method, GMM as exploratory supplement. Added Paper 4 (N=4,568) cross-domain reference. Added sample size caveat.

2. **Section title** — "Five Theoretically Derived Trajectory Patterns" → "Predicted Trajectory Patterns" with honest attribution: 3 theory-derived + 2 from EdNet exploratory analysis.

3. **Class 6 description** — Reframed as outlier phenomenon (n=2), excluded from substantive theoretical interpretation.

4. **Theoretical Pattern Mapping** — Class 6 retained for descriptive completeness only, not a distinct theoretical pattern.

5. **Discussion Summary** — Rewrote to lead with LCGA as robust primary finding. GMM as hypothesis-generating. Cross-domain convergence with EdNet highlighted.

6. **ABE section** — Added explicit Paper 4 numbers: EdNet ABE n=451 (gap≈-0.600) vs Chess ABE n=9 (gap=-0.600). Cross-domain credibility argument.

7. **Complementary Methods** — LCGA = primary/inferentially robust; GMM = exploratory. Future larger-sample confirmatory framing.

8. **Implications** — Reframed as "design hypotheses warranting further empirical testing" rather than established findings.

9. **Conclusion** — Softened from definitive claims to LCGA statistics + GMM as hypotheses for future confirmatory research.

10. **RQ3** — "theoretically predicted" → "predicted"

**Figures:** Reduced from 7 to 5 (removed 3D Theoretical Framework and Theory-vs-Empirical comparison — no real data backing).
